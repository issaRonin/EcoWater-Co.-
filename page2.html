Our Solution

To solve Somalia's water crisis

Our solution is to dig wells of water all across Somalia. The well water will be sourced from a variety of areas including springwater, ground water, and ocean water. However, this well water will be dirty and some of it will have salt in it. To fix this problem we will filter the water to make it safe for Somalians to drink with a combination of desalination (a filtration process that removes salt from water) and distillation (heating up water until it evaporates and then condesing it back into liquid form). However, this solution will have some downsides due to cost of equipment and the length of time it takes to build wells.

How this helps Somalian people

This will help Somalians in a number of ways. Firstly, it will make clean drinking water much more accesible to a number of Somalian citizens. This helps as not only does water preserve life but water is also used in numerous different tasks such as hygiene, and cooking. Making clean water more accesible to Somalian people will significantly reduce death rates of starvation and thirst.
