Background on Somalia

Somalia's Water Crisis

Somalia has had a major water and food crisis ever since facing their worst drought in 40 years. This drought combined with Somalia's arid weather caused many people to starve to death or die of thirst. This is an ongoing problem and we have a solution to make clean water significantly more accesible to Somalian citizens impacted by these tragic shortages.
